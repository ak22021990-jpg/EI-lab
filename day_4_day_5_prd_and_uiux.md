# 📅 DAY 4: Experience & Application Integrity

## 🎯 Objective
Train agents to investigate and validate candidate experience, application history, and data integrity across multiple sources **without making final hiring decisions**.

---

## 🧠 Learning Outcomes
- Identify duplicate applications
- Determine primary & first valid application
- Validate total and managerial experience
- Detect inconsistencies across resume, portal, and system
- Interpret candidate lifecycle (rehire, status, history)
- Reconstruct a clean, validated candidate profile

---

## 🚫 Out of Scope
- Final decisions (Proceed / Reject)
- Escalation flows
- Tool navigation training
- Visa/location compliance (covered in Day 5)

---

# 🎯 Scoring System

## Components
- Accuracy Score
- Completeness Score
- Risk Penalty

## Formula
Score = (Correct Findings / Total Findings) * 70 + Completeness Bonus * 20 - Critical Miss Penalty * 30
